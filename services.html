Our Construction Services

Services may be completed individually or combined into a complete structural-framing package.

Residential

Custom Home Framing

Complete framing for custom homes, including floor systems, exterior walls, interior walls, structural beams, roof systems, blocking, backing, and sheathing.

Multifamily

Apartments and Townhomes

Efficient framing for apartments, townhomes, duplexes, and other multifamily developments using organized and repeatable production methods.

Commercial

Light-Commercial Framing

Wood and light-gauge framing services for offices, retail buildings, tenant improvements, mixed-use developments, and other commercial structures.

Structural

Floor Systems

Installation of floor joists, engineered I-joists, beams, hangers, blocking, rim boards, subfloor sheathing, and associated structural connections.

Structural

Roof Systems

Roof truss installation, conventional rafter framing, overbuilds, roof sheathing, fascia framing, structural connections, and roof opening coordination.

Renovation

Additions and Remodels

Structural reframing, additions, new openings, temporary support, selective demolition, structural tie-ins, and repair of existing framing.

Specialty

Decks and Outdoor Structures

Structural framing for decks, porches, covered patios, pergolas, exterior stairs, and other outdoor construction projects.

Management

Project Coordination

Foreman oversight, material planning, daily production tracking, coordination with other trades, quality control, and punch-list completion.

Quality

Framing Inspection and Repair

Inspection of framing conditions, identification of construction defects, structural repair work, hardware corrections, and preparation for inspections.
